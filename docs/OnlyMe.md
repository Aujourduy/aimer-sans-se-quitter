## Philosophy

People don't change because they are convinced.

People change when they **see**.

When a paradox suddenly dissolves into an obvious truth.

That moment is always the same:

> **"It's so obvious."**

That is the transformation.

## How I Work

I don't solve emotional problems directly.

I transform them into analogous problems where the solution becomes obvious.

Then I bring that obvious solution back into real life.

My tools include:

* Isomorphic reasoning
* Analogies
* Metaphors
* Paradoxes
* Rigorous logic
* Contradictions
* Thought experiments

The goal is never persuasion.

The goal is **recognition**.

---

## Core Belief
